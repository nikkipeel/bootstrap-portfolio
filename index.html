Hi there!

My name is Nikki and I'm a Front End Web Developer. I'm experienced using HTML, CSS, Javascript (including ReactJS), Wordpress, and CSS frameworks such as Tailwind, Bootstrap, and Google's Materialize. I'm also experienced with using Adobe products such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and XD - which I use regularly for wireframing and prototyping designs.

As a disabled woman, I strive to design and develop websites with accessibility at the forefront. I utilize automated tools in my workflow to test for accessibility as well as manual testing for compliance with WCAG standards.

On most days, I can be found working on my current projects, creating new designs on Codepen, and advancing my technical skills. I'm currently seeking a remote role as a web developer. I'd love the opportunity to contribute my knowledge toward your project or collaborate with your development team!
